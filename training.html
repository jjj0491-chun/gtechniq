Training Courses

Gtechniq training courses are designed with Gtechniq fans, detailing enthusiasts, hobby detailers, and valeters in mind.

We offer a one or two-day course delivered at Gtechniq Works, Malaysia Gtechniq’s Centre of Excellence.

All courses are delivered by Alivn , Gtechniq Works detailing centre manager, who is highly regarded in the global detailing industry.  Alvin has been detailing for over 10 years and is the font of detailing knowledge behind Gtechniq.

At present Gtechniq are offering the following courses:
  • Total surface care -  one-day training course
  • Detailing foundation - two-day training course
  • Wash and maintenance club meet
