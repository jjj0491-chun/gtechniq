Show Season

By Gtechniq July 31, 2019 Latest News

Goodwood Festival of Speed’s theme this year was ‘Speed Kings, Motorsports Record Breakers’ and saw everything from the First Glance Paddock which held a world of debuts to the Goodwood Action Sport Arena where FMX and MTB riders took to the track to show off their tricks, an appearance from the Red Arrows, Aston Martin celebrated their 70th year racing at Goodwood and the Volkswagen ID R set a new Goodwood Hillclimb record of 41.18 seconds – so something for everyone!

Waxstock was a detailers paradise! Traders such as Rupes, Slim’s Detailing, Clean + Shiny and AMDetails had exceptional stands and the trade dinner was one to remember.

If you didn’t make it to either of these events, I’d say they’re both a definite must for 2020!
